# ANSG — Radiation Detector Simulations
<!---->
Geant4 simulations for studying semiconductor detector responses to alpha particles, gamma rays, and neutrons.
See individual subproject READMEs for details.
<!---->
## Subprojects
- **SiDiode** — Alpha energy deposition in a Si diode; studies effects of air and detector angle on peak shape.
- **Post-OSU** — Thermal neutron internal activation peaks in a CZT detector.
- **DTModeratorOptimization** — Finding the optimal polyethylene thickness to thermalize 14.1 MeV (DT) neutrons.
- **Prelim/CdTe, SiLi, CZT** — Detector response of CdTe, SiLi, and CZT to gamma rays from thermal neutron activation of Ge.
- **Prelim/GeometryOptimizationHPGe** — Optimizing the Ge sample geometry for producing the gamma rays used in the prelim detector studies.
